About SwaluWorks

Established in 2018, Sfyso Welding and Aluminium Works is a privately owned South African company based in Welkom, Free State. We specialise in manufacturing and installing aluminium products like windows, doors, garage doors, pivot doors, sliding and folding doors, shopfronts, and more.

We take pride in transforming homes with high-quality, affordable aluminium solutions. Whether you're replacing old wooden frames or building new, we help make your house look and feel brand new again.

Our work speaks for itself — we use only quality materials, and we’re committed to customer satisfaction. We serve clients throughout South Africa, mostly around the Free State.

Need a quote? Give us a call or invite us over — we’ll come do measurements and provide a free on-site quotation with no obligation.
